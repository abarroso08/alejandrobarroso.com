Publishing online often creates pressure to turn every thought into a performance. A quieter personal site offers another model: maintain a useful record, make it legible to others, and allow unfinished work to remain unfinished.

## A useful test

Before publishing, ask whether the page helps you remember, decide, explain, or connect. If it does none of those things, it may not need to exist.
